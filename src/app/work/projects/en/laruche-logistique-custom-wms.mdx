## Overview

At Planet Bourgogne, I first worked on ERP feature delivery for [Laruche Logistique](https://laruche-logistique.fr/), then co-built with a colleague a custom WMS as a dedicated product stream. I acted as the main developer, making some implementation decisions, and delivering core warehouse flows in a 6-week timeline for the V1.

This project was a great opportunity to take ownership of a critical business module and to learn how to deliver a useful product quickly under real constraints.

## Key Features

- **Core warehouse flows**: Implemented the initial operational workflows needed by the client.
- **Business adaptation**: Shaped functionality around the client's real warehouse constraints.
- **Delivery discipline**: Prioritized scope and sequencing to fit a 6-week timeline.
- **Team collaboration**: Coordinated implementation decisions with a colleague and stakeholders.

## Technologies Used

- **PHP 8 with custom framework**
- **GitLab workflow and code reviews**
- **Agile organization**

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a useful WMS quickly required hard trade-offs. The key learning was to focus on operational value first, keep architecture simple for V1, and avoid over-engineering under time pressure.

## Outcome

The first release enabled the client to start using a dedicated custom WMS workflow. It also gave me strong experience in fast product delivery and ownership of a critical business module.

The project is ongoing, and I continue to contribute to its evolution for over a year now.
